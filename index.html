About Renée

Renée Skibinski is an actor & voice actor in Atlanta, GA. She spent most of her childhood in Brazil & is fluent in Portuguese, & understands & speaks some Spanish. She began formal training with Mark Westbrook of ACS in Glasgow in 2013. She moved to Atlanta to pursue her career in acting in March 2015, continuing her training with Robert Mello. Renée has enjoyed performing with several improv troupes out of Highwire Comedy, as well as being a part of the Impulse Repertory Company.

I come from a multi-cultural family and background, which gave me a strong foundation in communication and cultivated a love for working with a team toward a common goal. I am fluent in Brazilian Portuguese and English, a lover of travel and grey, gloomy days.
