About me

Renée Skibinski is known for her leading role in “Go Mad and Mark.” You may also recognize her from the fantasy horror “Feed The Story Box,” which won BEST FEATURE at the Mistress Mary Horror Film Festival; From film-festival award-winning short film “Eden,” as well as recently starring in “The Mother,” currently in post-production.

Renée is an actor & voice actor in Atlanta, GA. She spent most of her childhood in Brazil & is fluent in Portuguese, & understands & speaks some Spanish. She began formal training with Mark Westbrook of ACS in Glasgow in 2013. She moved to Atlanta to pursue her career in acting in March 2015, continuing her training with Robert Mello. Renée has enjoyed performing with several improv troupes out of Highwire Comedy, as well as being a part of the Impulse Repertory Company.
